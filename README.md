# VonoTeam Business Process Analysis & PMIS Design

## Project Overview

Final project in Information Systems focused on analyzing and improving project management processes in a consulting organization managing multiple concurrent projects.

The project included business process analysis, requirements engineering, stakeholder interviews, workflow mapping, solution evaluation, and design of an improved project management environment.

## Project Objectives

- Analyze existing organizational processes
- Identify operational bottlenecks and inefficiencies
- Improve project visibility and workload management
- Support better managerial decision-making
- Design a centralized project management solution

## My Responsibilities

- Conducted stakeholder interviews with management and employees
- Performed workplace observations and process analysis
- Designed and analyzed BPMN process models
- Collected and documented business requirements
- Conducted user satisfaction surveys and analyzed findings
- Evaluated alternative PMIS solutions
- Designed future-state workflows and reporting structure
- Defined permissions, dashboards, alerts, and business rules

## Skills Demonstrated

- Business Analysis
- Requirements Engineering
- Process Mapping (BPMN)
- Systems Analysis
- Project Management
- Stakeholder Management
- Survey Analysis
- Solution Evaluation
- Information Systems Design

## Outcome

Designed a comprehensive project management solution aimed at improving project monitoring, resource allocation, workload visibility, reporting capabilities, and decision-making efficiency across the organization.
